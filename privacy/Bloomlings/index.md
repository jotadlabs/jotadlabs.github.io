# Privacy Policy — Bloomlings

**Last updated:** 2026-02-28

**Developer:** JOTAD Labs

## Overview
Bloomlings ("the App") is a mobile game developed by JOTAD Labs. We are committed to protecting your privacy and being transparent about the data we collect.

## Data We Collect

### With Your Consent
- **Analytics data**: Anonymous gameplay statistics (levels completed, play time, feature usage) to improve the game experience. Processed by Google Firebase Analytics.
- **Advertising data**: Ad interaction data for personalized or contextual advertising. Processed by Google AdMob.

### Automatically Collected
- **Device information**: Device model, OS version, screen size, and locale — used for compatibility and localization.
- **Crash reports**: Anonymous crash logs to identify and fix bugs.

### Data We Do NOT Collect
- Personal identification information (name, email, phone)
- Location data
- Contacts or photos
- Data from children under 13

## In-App Purchases
Purchase transactions are handled entirely by Apple App Store or Google Play Store. We do not collect or store payment information.

## Data Storage
- Game progress is stored locally on your device.
- No game data is uploaded to external servers unless analytics consent is granted.

## Your Rights
- **Opt out of analytics**: Decline the consent dialog on first launch, or toggle in Settings → Privacy.
- **Delete data**: Uninstalling the app removes all local data. For analytics data deletion, contact us.
- **Access your data**: Contact us to request a copy of any data associated with your device.

## Children's Privacy
Bloomlings does not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13 (COPPA) or under 16 (GDPR). The game does not require account creation or login.

## Third-Party Services
- Apple App Store (iOS purchases)
- Google Play Store (Android purchases)
- Google AdMob (advertising, with consent)
- Google Firebase Analytics (analytics, with consent)

## Changes to This Policy
We may update this privacy policy. Changes will be noted with an updated date at the top of this document and posted at the URL below.
