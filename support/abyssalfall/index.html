Frequently Asked Questions

The game crashes on startup.
Try restarting your device and launching again. If the issue persists, please contact us with your device model and OS version.

I was charged but didn't receive my purchase.
Please restore purchases using the in-game option. If the problem continues, contact us with your receipt and we'll resolve it promptly.

How do I request a refund?
For iOS, refunds are handled by Apple — visit reportaproblem.apple.com. For Android, contact Google Play support.
